Why Bees?

1 out of 3 bites of food have been polinated by bees.

Bees help produce 34% of the food that the US produces, such as almonds, avocadoes and grapes. Produce being pollinated by beesmakes up $15 billion of the economy, with these loses, America loses up to $2 billion annually. Without bees, there leads to be no food.
